Program Overview

This internship is not for the faint of heart. We provide an artistic approach to financial management, international bookkeeping, operations, and fiscal identity. We have a few distinct paths you can focus on. One path is very artistic as you help create blockchain visions and work closely with our Fiscal Identity teams who consult with new & old currencies around the world. The other path is more traditional, yet still utilizes automation and machine learning to do bookkeeping and double data entry for organizations large and small.

This internship is ideal for individuals who have a passion for numerology, accounting, and have a deep respect for the fiscal branch of life that holds all transactions together. Our partnerships with international businesses all you to work directly with the financial departments of businesses, bookkeping, and operations as they grow and innovate to stay ahead of the market. It is a fast paced, systematic, and forward-thinking team that is very tech-savvy and culturally diverse.
