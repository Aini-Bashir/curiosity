Program Overview

We are partnered with a Publishing House that has produced Best Sellers, coached authors to success, published books with people all over the world, and has over 20 years experience. We have developed newspapers, magazines, books, digital magazines, digital blog platforms, and so much more. During this program, you will work under the direction of the Publishing House's Senior Editor on a project of your choosing or get the opportunity to dabble in a variety of projects in the field of storytelling & publishing.

We use old fashioned think tank sessions along with tech-savvy cloud mashups where you collaborate with writers and publishing staff all over the world. We utilize emotional connection with our readers and use innovative frameworks in publishing so messages are ingested in the best formats possible. We have adapted with time to move from standard paperbacks & print to electronic format and we stay at the forefront of the industry by doing research and development into new modes of communication with our audience. This program will prepare you for a career in copywriting, desktop publishing, authorship, and other aspects of the publishing industry.
