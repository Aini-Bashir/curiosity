Ignite Your Curiosity

Follow these steps to join the Curiosity Community.
This will take a minimum of 40 hours. We take our community seriously and are looking for new members who are excited about education & changing the world!
01

Complete Orientation

Learn the basics to our community along with our learning philosophy. Gain Life & Skills to be enthusiastic and useful for any subject as you increase your AQ, EQ, and IQ.
02

Join the Community

Register Online to ignite your curiosity with us. Meet people from all over the world and see our digital world come to life.
03

Pick a Learning Path

Gain technical skills and find a focus to increase your curiosity score, which is shared with businesses around the world. This score is performance-based and and our indicator of success.
04

Make Connections

As you interact with the Curiosity Community, you will naturally gain inside access to multiple pathways in life: whether that is job shadowing, internships, or career options. The more soft skills and tech skills you build, the more you will get noticed and asked to join various hives within the network.

Gain Access to the Curiosity Community: Take the Curiosity Quiz after you have completed Orientation.

Before downloading this software, make sure you have properly registered and you have completed the Welcome Class. After you have completed your welcome quiz, you are able to join one of our Hives where you can finally socialize with everyone!

The Community

The Curiosity Communitye is where people from all over the world come together to learn, collaborate, chat, and socialize on projects together. It is the central hub to Ignite your Curiosity where you get to watch experts, participate in large projects that have an impact on the entire world, and meet like-minded students from all parts of the globe.

We have strong initiatives in USA, Pakistan, Africa, China, France, Costa Rica, and are continuing to expand our outreach in STEAM education.
