WHY STEAM? ENROLL

We Make STEAM Cool!

STEAM is an educational approach to learning that uses the top fields needed in the workforce as access points for guiding student inquiry, dialogue, and critical thinking. We don't just teach it: We Make STEAM Cool!

As a system of organized curiosity science is knowledge also known as the system of knowledge. Science converts general truths, the operation of general laws as obtained and tested through scientific methods.

The application of tools, materials and knowledge to solve problems and extend human capabilities.

The application of science and mathematics by which the properties of matter and the sources of energy in nature are made useful to society in an organized fashion.

The conscious use of skill and creative imagination with international impact across the senses.

Knowledge that deals with the logic of shape, quantity and arrangement. Math is all around us, in everything we do. It is the building block for everything in our daily lives. The needs of math arose based on the wants of society. The more complex a society, the more complex the mathematical needs. Primitive tribes needed little more than the ability to count, but also relied on math to calculate the position of the sun and the physics of hunting.

Our Mission

Ignite Global Curiosity

We are the first P-20+ STEAM Initiative where we enrich the lives of entire communities through education. We bring the love of learning to remote areas with hive mentality and our cloud friendly digital platform.

Ignite Your Curiosity

The SMILE Method™

The Self-Motivated Impact Learning Environment Method utilizes techniques for success in learning.

CURIOSITY CAMPS

For years, we have created bootcamp style retreats where students learn innovative ways to get involved in STEAM and use high-tech computers. Here, we teach our pedagogy and implementation.

CURIOSITY COMMUNITY

This global community is comprised of a large cloud platform, amazing analytical tools to measure engagement, and multiple hives where participants socialize and collaborate together.

SMILE SPACES

A quiet and peaceful live-in education center where students guide their own learning as they explore areas in mental health, well-being, and hopefully find curiosity towards STEAM education.

Ways To Be Curious

Personalized & Self-Motivated

Use the SMILE Method™

Spark creativity and passion inside yourself and join our community 100% free. Join like-minded people from around the world who are collaborating to start new beginnings and discover the wonders of the world.

Group Coaching

Join the Focus Hive

In this approach, you will pay a monthly fee and be placed in a hive along with a few dozen other members where you will experience Agile methodology on a Scrum for remote work & learning while you discover your passions and new skills.

1-on-1 Private Lessons

Do Curiosity Coaching

Work with a Certified Curiosity Coach for one-on-one learning over the internet where we custom-tailor lessons to the career and future you desire. Our coaches are trained in a variety of software and innovativ teaching styles.
