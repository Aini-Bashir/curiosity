How To Volunteer

Your little time and patience is all we need. Your useless items are important to others.
01

Complete Orientation

Many students around the world struggle with technology and with motivation. Our volunteers are the opposite of this: being tech-savvy, passionate for learning & teaching, and exhibiting a high Curiosity Score by implementing the SMILE Method™. Complete Orientation as your first step.
02

Join A Project

After fully understanding our system, announce your desires to volunteer inside the Curiosity Community. If your Curiosity Score is high enough, Project Managers will naturally reach out to you and ask you to join on various projects that are on-going.
03

Share Our Mission

Spread the awareness and let people join us. Share our digital link with people willing to help.
